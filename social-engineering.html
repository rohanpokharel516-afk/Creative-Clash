What is Social Engineering?

The practice of manipulating individuals into divulging confidential information or performing actions that compromise security by exploiting human trust.

How to Prevent It

  • Be skeptical of unsolicited requests for private data.
  • Verify identities before responding to unusual demands.
  • Do not share passwords or OTPs, even if someone claims to be from support.
  • Report suspicious interactions within your organisation or to authorities.
