Unsecured Wi‑Fi

Risks of public wireless networks

Home Digital Rights Online Safety Cyber Threats

What is it?

Open or poorly secured Wi‑Fi networks can be monitored by attackers, allowing them to intercept data you send or receive, including passwords and personal information.

How to Prevent It

  • Avoid logging into sensitive accounts when connected to public Wi‑Fi.
  • Use a trusted VPN to encrypt your traffic.
  • Prefer mobile data or secure networks for banking and email.
  • Turn off automatic connections to open networks.
