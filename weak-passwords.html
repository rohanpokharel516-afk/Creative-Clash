Weak / Reused Passwords

Why you need strong, unique passwords

Home Digital Rights Online Safety Cyber Threats

What is it?

Using simple or the same password across multiple accounts makes it easy for hackers to gain access once one password is compromised.

How to Prevent It

  • Create long, complex passwords with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • Use a different password for each service.
  • Consider a reputable password manager to store and generate passwords.
  • Change passwords regularly and after any suspected breach.
